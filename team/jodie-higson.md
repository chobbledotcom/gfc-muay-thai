      # Jodie Higson

      ## Coach

      Jodie coaches at GFC and runs the gym with Luke Phillips. In 2024 they reopened GFC at Bright Street Mill after members gave their time to help with the building work.

      Jodie has also spoken about the ladies-only sessions and the pressures of fitting training around work and family life. Women do not need to be fit or experienced before they start; everyone can work at their own pace.
