      # Luke Philips

      ## Coach

      **Luke Phillips was in Thai Boxing gyms before he could walk, learning from the very start, he was training before he was three. This early start means he has already over 25 years experience in Muay Thai. A highly decorated junior competitor, he was unbeaten as an adult fighter before retiring from competition to concentrate on teaching. Now the head instructor at GFC Luke has spent a lot of time in Thailand, living in Muay Thai camps alongside the Thais. His knowledge picked up there together with his training in England makes him already one of the best instructors around.**
