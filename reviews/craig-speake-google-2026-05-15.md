Fantastic place to start or refresh your muay thai journey. My son has been training here for around 7 years now and has built up excellent skills both physically and mentally.  From a day one beginner to travelling abroad becoming a world champion speaks highly about the level of coaching that is provided here! My daughter also trains here and has developed skills and confidence that will certainly help out in the future. An excellent atmosphere with a family friendly approach makes you feel incredibly welcome and included. I would highly recommend GFC to anyone that wants to learn muay thai or just wants to take to a hobby to get fitter.
