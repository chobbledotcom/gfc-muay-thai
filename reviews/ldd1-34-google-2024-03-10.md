Trained at several boxing and martial arts gyms in the past. GFC is one the best I’ve been too. They have it set up with advanced and beginner sessions, where one of the top fighters (Kieran, Lewis, Dan) will coach the beginners. Everyone is incredibly welcoming and friendly. There seems to be a big no ego policy as the top fighters and head coach are really nice people. Everyone here seems to want to better each other and appear more like a close friend group than just people training together. Genuinely was a pleasure to train here and will be back in the future.
